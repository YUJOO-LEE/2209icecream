CEO'S MESSAGE

YUJOO's Ice Cream makes all of its mixes from wholesome ingredients. Fresh cream and condensed milk are delivered daily to the manufacturing plant. To ensure the best tasting product is produced, YUJOO's uses the highest quality of cocoa available, anywhere. The fruit flavors are made from hand-picked, fresh-frozen fruits. YUJOO's only uses high grade almonds, pecans and walnuts in their recipes. Additionally, the nuts are roasted on site to guarantee freshness and quality, every time!
